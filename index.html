Dosun-Fuwari puzzle

Rules

  • The grid represents a side view: the bottom is the floor and the top is the ceiling. Black blocks are solid.
  • Your goal is to place an anvil and a balloon on each of the groups of cells (delimited by thicker lines).
  • Anvils are heavy and go down: they can only be placed on the floor, on a black block or on another anvil.
  • Ballons are light and go up: they can only be placed under the ceiling, under a black block or under another balloon.
